HOW TO MAKE A MICROWAVE HOTDOG

Ingredients

  • A hotdog

  • A microwave

  • A knife

Instructions

  1. Take hotdog out of package.

  2. Place hotdog on paper towel.

  3. Cut three holes in hotdog to allow steam to disperce.

  4. Wrap paper towel around hotdog.

  5. Place hotdog in the microwave.

  6. Set and start microwave for 30 seconds.

  7. Feel hotdog to make sure it is warm.

  8. Enjoy your hotdog.
